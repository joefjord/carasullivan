Cara Sullivan

Instructor and Educational Designer at The Delta School

I am a teaching artist. I am currently an instructor at The Delta School, where cutting edge project design and strengths-based learning are a passion. I have sought breadth in my teaching over the years, having taught Foundations in Visual Art at Arkansas State University for many years, and before that teaching all age groups at the Charlott Jones School of Art, and the Foundation of Arts before that.

Born in Maine, I received my undergraduate degree from Arkansas State University, and my graduate degree from Rutgers, The State University of New Jersey by way of the Belfast School of Art, Northern Ireland. I have exhibited in Belfast, Northern Ireland, Brooklyn, NY, Memphis, TN, St. Louis, MO, Little Rock, AR, and have work in the permanent collection of the Historic Arkansas Museum. For me, art is as much an intellectual pursuit as it is a creative one.

Every summer I open my personal studio for week-long art camps! This is a unique experience for students to create art in the same space as a professional artist, one who makes a point to listen closely to each child to help them realize their strengths. This is evidenced in the outcomes, which are unique to each child—they don’t finish the week all having made the same painting. When children are truly engaged, they get “in the zone” a flow state that allows them to be incredibly productive and less distracted. At the end of each camp, students will have one of their creations framed and ready to hang at home!

Each week long, two-hour camp is $170, and one-hour camps for the little ones are $85. There is a nominal supply fee that does not cover our costs, but helps offset them. We provide all supplies, which are of high quality. There is no fee for Kinder - 1st grade classes.

A sibling discount of 10% will be applied to the second registrant from a given family (must be a sibling). The framing of one of your child's artworks is included in the cost of all camps! Wow! A half tuition payment should be made to reserve your child’s spot, with the remainder of tuition due on the first day of the session. Additionally, any child signing up for more than one session will receive a 10% discount for each session after the first.
