Cara Sullivan

Artist and Instructor of Foundations at AState

I teach full time in the Department of Art + Design at ASTATE, but many of you know me from the Charlott Jones School of Art, and the Foundation of Arts before that. I love the creative minds of children and am looking forward to what a blast these art camps will be!

I'm opening my home studio for young students during the Summer months for week long art camps! This will be a unique experience for students to create art in the same space as a professional artist, and to be guided through a range of delightful, creative projects in a variety of media. At the end of each camp, students will have one of their creations framed and ready to hang at home!

Click the button above to e-mail me your request for a given session(s) and I’ll pencil you in! The Summer Studio will bill you via e-mail. Initial payments (to reserve a spot) or payments in full can be made by check, PayPal or Cash App. Any outstanding billed amounts are due on the first day of camp. Payments in person may also be made with the aforementioned methods or with cash.
